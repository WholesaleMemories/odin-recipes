Grilled Cheese

Description

I mean, it's grilled cheese. Crispy, buttery, and gooey. Who doesn't love it?

Ingredients

  • 2 slices White Bread
  • 2 slices American Cheese
  • 2 tbs Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Bring a non-stick pan up to heat over medium heat.
  2. Melt the butter to grease the pan.
  3. Assemble the sandwich by placing both slices of cheese between the slices of bread.
  4. Fry the sandwich in the pan - 2 minutes per side.
  5. Enjoy!
